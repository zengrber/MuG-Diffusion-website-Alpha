Special Settings

Special settings include 2 parameters:


  • 1.minimal interval for eliminating minijacks: You can input number directly in the textbox to set the interval.
    This number means that MuG-Diffusion will will rearrange patterns to reduce intractable minijack less than the given interval. By default, 90 ms is the 1/4 meter of 166.67 bpm. This interval can be calculated from the formula below: 

    Interval = 15,000 / song BPM

  • 2.Snap notes to grids: Tick snap notes to grids box to enable this setting.
    This setting will allow Mug-Diffusion to automatically align generated notes to the bar lines, making the chart look more well-organized. It is recommended to turn on this setting under the premise of unchanged BPM.
